Assessment Rubric

Criteria Well Above Standard Above Standard At Standard Approaching Standard Below Standard
AI Literacy Uses AI expertly and strategically to support learning and creation. Uses AI effectively with clear purpose. Shows evidence of editing. Uses AI adequately for research or drafting. Uses AI inconsistently or without purpose. Over-relies on unedited AI content.
Critical Understanding Demonstrates insightful evaluation of AI's responses. Analyses AI output thoughtfully. Describes AI output with some simple evaluative comments. Accepts AI output largely at face value. Shows no evaluation of AI output.
Conceptual Understanding Comprehensive and accurate understanding of the concept. Clear and mostly accurate understanding. Basic understanding, explanations mostly correct. Limited or partial understanding. Inaccurate or minimal understanding.
Ethical Reasoning Insightful reflection on ethical use of AI. Thoughtful reflection on how AI was used. Brief mention of ethics or credit. Mentions ethics superficially. No ethical reflection.
Judgement and Curation Consistently selects and refines AI outputs with strong discernment. Selects appropriate AI outputs and edits them effectively. Uses AI outputs with some basic editing. Uses AI outputs with minimal editing. Copies AI outputs without evaluation.
Creativity and Integration Creates a highly engaging, original presentation. Clear, well-structured, and engaging presentation. Presentation is clear but predictable. Lacks clear structure or originality. Presentation incomplete or disorganized.
